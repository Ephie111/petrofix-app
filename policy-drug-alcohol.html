Drug & Alcohol Policy

PETROFIX is committed to ensuring safe and productive working environments for all employees. Management acknowledges that employees' use of alcohol, drugs, or other substances may hinder their work performance and affect the safety, efficiency, and reputation of the company.

This policy is applicable to all employees of PETROFIX, temporary workers, subcontractors, and includes activities conducted on company or client premises. An exception is allowed for the lawful possession and appropriate usage of prescribed medicinal drugs, provided they are specifically assigned by a qualified healthcare provider, used strictly according to prescription guidelines, do not impede performance, and the employee keeps their supervisor informed.

Policy Requirements

  • We maintain a zero-tolerance stance regarding drugs and alcohol.
  • Employees are expected to perform their duties without being under the influence of alcohol or any other substances that could impair their ability to work safely and legally.
  • The use, distribution, possession or sale of illegal and controlled substances on Company premises or when on company business is strictly prohibited.
  • Employees are forbidden from working while under the influence of drugs or alcohol; anyone suspected of impairment will be reported, immediately suspended pending an investigation, and removed from the workplace.

Management Authority

  • Perform random testing on employees within the workplace at regular intervals.
  • Deny entry to the workplace to any individuals suspected of being under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
  • Implement disciplinary measures against any employee whose job performance is affected by the illegal use of drugs or alcohol.
Signed by Management  •  2025
