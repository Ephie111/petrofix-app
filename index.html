Who We Are

Built for the
Energy Sector

Petrofix Energy Limited is a dynamic and forward-thinking oil and gas service company based in Nigeria, delivering high-impact technical solutions and manpower services across the energy value chain.

Our approach combines industry best practices with deep local expertise to deliver value-driven results. Whether supporting upstream development, optimizing production, or supplying expert personnel — we are committed to operational excellence.

With a focus on Nigeria and the wider West African region, we serve as a trusted partner for national and international energy companies alike.

How We Deliver

Petrofix IPM Model

Our Integrated Project Management model covers the complete oil/gas well life-cycle — from Project Definition through Production to Work-over/P&A — maximizing NPV while maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality.

  • Holistic Lifecycle Management — from conceptual design to commissioning and operations
  • Drilling Risk Assessment — integrated throughout early project phases
  • Cost Optimization — reducing capital expenditure while maintaining quality
  • Technology Integration — leveraging digital tools for planning and monitoring
  • Stakeholder Engagement — clients, communities, and employees involved throughout
