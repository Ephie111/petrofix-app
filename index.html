Who We Are

Built for the
Energy Sector

Petrofix Energy Limited is a dynamic and forward-thinking oil and gas service company based in Nigeria, delivering high-impact technical solutions and manpower services across the energy value chain.

Petrofix Energy delivers integrated technical, operational, and commercial solutions across Africa, the Middle East, and global energy markets, combining local expertise with international experience to maximize asset value and operational efficiency.

With a focus on Nigeria and globally, we serve as a trusted partner for national and international energy companies.
